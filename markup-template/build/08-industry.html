Training and Consulting for Your Unique Organization

We offer a suite of training programs that are always customized to your combination of class size and industry. These multi-day, onsite training workshops will help your company mitigate risk and improve performance thanks to our hands-on, best-in-class instructors.

Our training and consulting experiences cover a wide variety of topics relevant to high-risk industries including Root Cause Analysis (RCA), Management Oversight and Risk Tree (MORT), and Safety Culture Assessment. We are even equipped to train for Nuclear Material Handling.

Markets We Serve

Oil, Gas & Chemical

Our Root Cause Analysis training is a key component for oil, gas, and chemical companies that are looking to improve performance and safety management in fields that have no tolerance for errors. We combine our industry knowledge and training acumen to help these companies improve profitability and problem-solving.

Energy Utilities

Whether it’s fossil, nuclear, or electric, energy utilities must always be able to maintain a steady supply to cities and municipalities. Our risk mitigation services help to improve performance and profitability while also providing learning opportunities through root cause analysis.

Manufacturing & Engineering

Delays cost time and money and can often affect a company’s culture if they become persistent. With our Management Oversight and Risk Tree training, we can help manufacturers and engineers keep projects safe, on time, and profitable.

Agriculture, Food & Beverage

For food and beverage producers, a steady supply chain with 100% consumer trust is vital. Reduce product recalls, improve performance and enhance culture with quality root cause analysis at every step of the chain.

Government and Regulatory Bodies

C-E also provides beginning-to-end support services and tools for government oversight, support, and regulatory agencies including HAZMAT, nuclear services and the Bureau of Safety and Environmental Enforcement (BSEE).

State Government

Agencies and commissions for public utilities, safety services, and disaster relief have very real challenges and responsibilities to your citizens. With you, Conger-Elsea will mitigate risk, enhance culture and improve performance for the people and businesses of your state.

Federal Government

With experience working with government agencies from the Department of Transportation and Environmental Protect Agency to the Pipeline and Hazardous Materials Safety Administration and U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission, we are equipped to train you on maintaining relationships and compliance across the federal landscape.

International Government

Relationships across borders require a seasoned partner who you can trust and expect to deliver results. Conger-Elsea is passionate about our space, and passionate about qualified support and assistance for you and your government, no matter where you are, around the world. We have worked with the Canadian Nuclear Safety Commission, International Atomic Energy Agency, the European Union and more.

Aerospace & Defense

Working with government contracts can bring an entire separate host of regulatory concerns with no margin for error. Our root cause analysis and Management Oversight and Risk Tree training help improve safety, reliability, and compliance for aerospace, defense, and other government endeavors.

Legal and Professional Services

A misstep in the legal world can often result in heavy fines, lawyer fees, and more. Our consultants can help your company expand its portfolio of clients and services through our decades of risk and incident investigation consulting experience.
