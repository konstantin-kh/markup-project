30 Years of Service Excellence.

For more than three decades Conger & Elsea is recognized as the trusted global leader for premier, certified training and consulting solutions and services for: (RCA) Root Cause Analysis / Incident Investigation, (CAP) Corrective Action Program, Safety / Safety Culture Training, Regulatory Compliance, and Education, and Human Performance Improvement.

With a refined emphasis on MORT Training (Management Oversight and Risk Tree), our specialized team of industry-focused professionals use this comprehensive, analytical discipline as a method for determining risk, and the causes and contributing factors of incidents.

Conger & Elsea develops a rich understanding of each client’s business or project and then applies insight, skills and the resources required to address relevant, industry-specific issues and opportunities.
